For Frankie

For Frankie is a free laundry service that was started by Hannah's friend and bridesmaid, Adahlia Nix. Every Wednesday, Adah + her volunteers meet at a laundromat and provide quarters, laundry detergent, and other essentials to those in need. The sense of community For Frankie fosters is what makes this organization so near and dear to Hannah's heart. To learn more about For Frankie, click here. Be sure to follow Adah in her journey with For Frankie on Facebook and Instagram.

Project Host

Project host is a Greenville-area soup kitchen, culinary school, and catering company. As an organization, Project Host embodies the idea that Chris's parents instilled in him growing up -- that, despite one's circumstances, everyone deserves the chance to succeed. By offering training and job opportunities for those who may not have had that chance otherwise, Project Host not only makes a immediate change, but a lasting one as well.
